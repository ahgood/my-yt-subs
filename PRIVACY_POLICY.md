# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-17

My YT Subscriptions (the "Extension") does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## Data Access
- The Extension requests access to https://www.youtube.com/feed/channels to read your subscription list when you open the overlay.
- The Extension does not send this data to any external server.

## Data Storage
- The Extension does not store subscription data locally or remotely.
- All data is fetched on demand each time the overlay is opened.

## Analytics
- The Extension does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
